FrndX - TheEvent

A Social Travel App to find travelmates and potential lifelong friends!
A platform dedicated for interested travellers!

No more travelling alone!
The idea behind this platform is inspired by my own experience.

Even though we have a variety of social media apps or messaging platforms in the market, but it's always tiresome and difficult reaching out to friends (because they're either busy doing work, studying or enjoying with others) and convince them to travel whenever you want to. Thus, you end up travelling alone. Time mismatch :(

Therefore, this platform helps you not rely on your friends and tries to bridge the gap between you and other interested people around the city which might foster into a good friendship later on.

A platform to find or connect with other travelmates.

  • Features daily public groups that people can create or instead join one if they're going to the same place!
  • Helps you travel to unknown places / events (that others know, but you don't) and make new friends (who love to travel) while travelling.
  • Filter groups according to time of the day, budget, proximity, location etc.

After joining the travel group, introduce yourselves in the CHAT room and decide upon a place to meet before beginning your travel. Features include (but not limited to):

  • POLL (majority votes) functionality for a meeting place.
  • LIVE GPS tracking of group members to easily locate your travelmates (with a privacy feature).

Auto-Match Notification System.

Using AI and Data Science, a pop-up notification functionality to recommend places or let you know

  • If someone else is travelling to your favorite place.
  • If any of your friends are travelling.
  • If there is any cool event going on near you.
NOTE: The plaform will also show how many of your friends are currently chilling out in a particular place (with privacy mode on/off, of course!).

Take a quick glance at the place you're gonna visit beforehand.

Let your curiosity increase!

All in one. Explore the best places to visit.

  • Filters and recommends a list of places to be covered in your given time and budget.
  • Shows the total amount before travelling (including entrance fees, taxi fares etc.).

No more fretting over what places to visit and googling fees and taxi fares separately.

Not only UAE, but the WORLD!

I believe this platform if worked seriously on and with the right mentorship, will have the capability to influence our busy modern society (including high school and university students, and adults as well) and enhance our social life. The integration of AI and Data Science into this platform will give it an extra ability and edge to work and provide results according to our exact needs, thus saving us time and effort.

It'll especially be a boon for:

  • Tourists: To find native people who can travel and guide them alongside, explaining the significances of those places.
  • People who find it hard to make friends or want to make new ones (by a new way).
